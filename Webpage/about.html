Pod is an application that allows you to control your house remotely. It is designed to allow you to transform your home into a 'smart house'.


It can be used in combination with your smart phone, allowing you to check whether doors/windows are locked and also whether the heating is on. You can also check forecasts and use these to either turn the heating on/off.
